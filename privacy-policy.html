SubSense Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2024

1. Data Collection and Usage

SubSense is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All subscription data, including:

  • Subscription names
  • Amounts
  • Renewal dates
  • Categories

is stored exclusively on your device.

2. Data Storage and Security

Your subscription information is stored securely on your device using industry-standard encryption. The data is accessible only through the SubSense app and is protected by your device's security features, including optional biometric authentication.

3. Data Protection

We implement several measures to protect your data:

  • Local storage with device-level encryption
  • Optional iCloud sync with Apple's security standards
  • No third-party access to your data
  • No external servers storing your information

4. User Rights

As a SubSense user, you have complete control over your data:

  • Access all your data directly within the app
  • Delete your data at any time through the app's settings
  • Export your data in a readable format
  • Control privacy settings within the app

5. Third-Party Services

SubSense operates independently and does not integrate with any third-party services for data collection, analytics, or advertising purposes. Your data remains isolated within your device.
